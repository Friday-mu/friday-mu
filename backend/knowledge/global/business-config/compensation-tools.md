# Compensation Tools

## Available Perks

| Perk | Condition | Host Cost | Guest Value | Best For |
|------|-----------|-----------|-------------|----------|
| Late checkout | Calendar clear (no same-day check-in) | €0 | €40 | Gap days, off-season, closing hesitant guests |
| Early check-in | Calendar clear (no same-day checkout) | €0 | €25-40 | Early flights, families |
| Free extra night | Low season or gap night | ~€20 cleaning | Full nightly rate | Near checkout, long-stay sweetener |
| Cleaning fee waiver | Margin allows | €20-40 | €30-60 | Gap-night bookings, multi-unit deals |
| Percentage discount | Within authority cap | Varies | Varies | Strong-case negotiation |
| Future stay credit | 120% of cash value | 75% never redeemed | 120% perceived | Loyalty building |

## Not Available

Dinners, restaurant vouchers, welcome baskets, airport transfers, private chef, wine, romance packs, beach gear, kids' fun packs, partner vouchers, or any perk requiring out-of-pocket spend with complex owner accounting splits.

## Strategy

Perks before cash discount when perk cost < 1/3 of cash ask. Discount only when dealEvaluation signals strong case, close-the-deal moment, never opener.

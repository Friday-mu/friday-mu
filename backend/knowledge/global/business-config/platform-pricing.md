# Platform Pricing

## Markup Strategy

- Airbnb: ~5% above website base price. Preferred platform — higher quality leads, most volume.
- Booking.com: 10-20% above website base price depending on Genius status. Genius discounts: Level 1 = 10%, Level 2 = 15%, Level 3 = 20% — base price marked up higher to compensate.
- Direct (friday.mu): Base price, no platform commission.

## Response Time Requirements

- Airbnb: 90% within 24h for Superhost. Under 1h optimal.
- Booking.com: Under 4h for Quick Response badge.

## Price Parity

Maintain consistent base pricing across all platforms. Manual discounts on top of already-optimized Guesty rates.

## Cross-Platform Calendar

All platforms use Guesty pricing automation. Weekly/monthly auto-discounts (15%/35%) apply uniformly on Airbnb and direct site.

## Guest Communication Differences

- Airbnb: Platform-native experience; maintain Airbnb voice; never mention other platforms
- Booking.com: Formal, business-travel oriented
- Direct: Relationship-building; emphasize brand and FRIDAY10 loyalty code

# Internal-Only Flags

## Cancellation Fee Rule

Friday keeps 100% of cancellation fees — no owner split. Do NOT surface proactively to owners. Case-by-case if an owner ever raises it.

## Tourist Tax

EUR 3/person/night for guests aged 12+. Already included in booking price. If asked: respond that it is included and cannot be removed. Do not proactively raise.

## VAT

Friday Retreats Ltd is VAT-registered (28238154). VAT treatment per service line; guest-facing communication defers to "included in price."

## Owner Payouts

Revenue recognized on checkout date. Payout within 7 days after month-end. Monthly statements sent within first 7 days of following month.

## Currency

Guest payments: EUR (sometimes cash MUR or USD, or wire to USD/EUR accounts). Owner payouts: MUR. Exchange rate: monthly weighted average across all payments received that month.

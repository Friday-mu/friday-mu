# Tax, VAT, And Tourist-Fee Caveats

## MRA Tourist Fee Anchor

MRA states that a tourist fee applies to eligible tourist accommodation from 1 October 2025 and is charged per tourist aged 12 or above per night, with exemptions listed by MRA. Ask Friday must not generalize this into final accounting treatment without finance review.

Operational implication:

- Use MRA as the official source for applicability, exemption classes, registration, return, and filing/payment timing.
- Refresh this source row monthly during rollout because fee rules, forms, and return guidance can change.
- Treat tourist-fee handling as a finance/compliance workflow, not a guest-support guess.
- Owner/guest wording needs reviewed Friday policy before publication.

## VAT And Tax Handling

- Use current MRA sources and Friday finance workpapers before making VAT/tax claims.

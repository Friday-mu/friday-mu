# Learning Analyzer

## Brand-Voice Scoring Criteria

Evaluate drafts against these principles:

1. **Specific-not-flexible** — Concrete numbers, exact dates, named referents?
2. **Commit-dont-dangle** — Decision or immediate next step? No dangling questions?
3. **Verify-before-committing** — Ops-dependent commitments paired with verification task?
4. **Never-fabricate-specifics** — All facts backed by canonical sources?
5. **Errors-handled-with-grace** — Brief acknowledgment, then forward?
6. **Address-whole-context** — All open concerns addressed, not just latest message?
7. **Gratitude-framed-private-feedback** — Private feedback acknowledged with gratitude?
8. **Close-with-warmth** — Hospitality comms close with warmth?
9. **Language-matching** — Guest's preferred language detected and matched?
10. **Temporary-mitigation-paired** — Mitigation grounded in canonical data, paired with team commitment?

## Drafting Discipline Checks

- Length matched to substance?
- Proper punctuation (no em-dashes)?
- Prose paragraphs default (bullets only for procedural)?
- Direct opening (no filler)?
- Inline information delivery (no deferral wrapping)?

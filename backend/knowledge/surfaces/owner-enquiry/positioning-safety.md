# Owner Positioning Safety

Use the staff-private matrix in `docs/architecture/ask-friday-owner-positioning-source-matrix-2026-05-29.md`.

## Allowed Direction

- Explain Friday's approved service model.
- Ask better qualification questions from market/competitor research.
- Mention that the team can review the property and propose the right operating model.
- Use official source-dated context for licensing/tourist-fee topics when approved.

## Not Allowed Without Review

- "We earn more than competitor X."
- "We are cheaper than competitor X."
- "You will make X per year."
- "We guarantee occupancy."
- "We handle all tax/legal/licensing obligations" unless approved wording says exactly that.
- Named competitor claims beyond approved wording.

## Safe Competitor Response

If asked about a named competitor, answer with Friday's approved model and scope differences, then offer a staff review. Do not invent competitor pricing, service quality, customer results, or legal/compliance claims.

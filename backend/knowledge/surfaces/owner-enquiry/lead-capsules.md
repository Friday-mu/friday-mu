# Owner Lead Capsule Rules

## Ready Lead Minimum

A lead is ready for staff follow-up when it has:

- owner name or contact identity,
- at least one contact method,
- property type or rough category,
- location/area,
- rough size/bedrooms/rooms if available,
- current pain point or goal,
- timing/urgency or follow-up intent.

## Do Not Over-Collect

If the owner has provided the ready-lead minimum, stop asking broad discovery questions. Summarize the capsule and route staff follow-up.

## Capsule Fields

- `ownerName`
- `contact`
- `propertyType`
- `area`
- `size`
- `currentChannelsOrManager`
- `painPoints`
- `goals`
- `timing`
- `sourceConversationSummary`
- `recommendedNextStep`
- `riskFlags`

## Privacy

Owner lead details are owner-scoped/staff-private. They are not public learning memory.

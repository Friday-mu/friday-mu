# Refund Bounds

## Hard Blocks

- Refund cost above €1000
- Guest injury reported: stop all compensation, collect evidence, no admission
- Legal threat: cease engagement, LEGAL_HOLD task only
- Multi-listing relocation: Ishant oversight required

## Severity Bands

| Band | Compensation | Mechanism |
|------|-------------|----------- |
| Minor (cosmetic, brief, low impact) | Up to 5% nightly rate per affected night | A + paired notify |
| Moderate (core amenity, several hours) | 10-30% nightly rate per affected night | A + paired notify |
| Severe (uninhabitable, full night lost) | 100% affected nights + relocation | A if ≤€1000, else B |

## Decision Process

1. Confirm guest explicitly requests compensation
2. Diagnose: what happened, how many nights affected
3. Apply standard metric (severity band percentage)
4. Assess owner fairness
5. Offer fix first (re-clean, repair, alternative)
6. Determine envelope and mechanism
7. Draft with mandatory Ishant-Slack-notify paired task

## Core Principles

- Guest-centered, owner-fair
- Underpromise and overdeliver
- Ishant must be notified on Slack for every refund
- Every case logged with reason, amount, dates, outcome
- Do everything possible to avoid negative reviews without being unreasonable

# Discount Bounds

## AI Envelope

AI operates within the same envelope as the human team. Every draft is human-reviewed before send.

## Hard Blocks

- Discount crossing Guesty floor price
- Net revenue negative after all costs
- Direct long-stay >1000 EUR GBV requiring additional discount

## Action Guidelines

| Case | Action | Mechanism |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Strong (low occupancy, high GBV, long stay, repeat, direct) | Up to €30 discount or perks | A (autonomous draft) |
| Moderate (mixed factors) | Perks or up to €15 | A; if guest pushes → B |
| Weak (few positives, rating concerns) | Perks only | A (perks only) |
| Below floor / net negative | Hard block | B (management check) |

## Perk Preference Order

1. Late checkout (€0 cost if no same-day check-in)
2. Early check-in (€0 if no same-day checkout)
3. Cleaning fee waiver (€20-40 cost)
4. Free extra night (€20 cleaning cost)

## Platform Context

- Last-minute (0-3 days): Already auto-reduced -30% to -40%. Manual discount 5-10% max.
- Gap nights: Already auto-reduced -15% to -30%. Prefer perks.
- Long stay (7+/28+): Already auto-reduced 15%/35%. Direct >1000 EUR → Mechanism B.

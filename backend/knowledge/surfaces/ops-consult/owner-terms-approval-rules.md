# Owner Terms, Expense Approval, And Service Recovery

Source: Friday.mu Property Management Terms & Conditions, live checked 2026-05-26:
https://www.friday.mu/fr-terms-and-conditions

This is an operational summary, not legal advice. If the signed property management agreement has property-specific exceptions, use that agreement over the default summary.

## Authority

Friday Retreats Ltd is appointed to market, operate, and manage the managed properties. The manager's authority includes powers set in the terms, Clause 9 maintenance/service recovery powers, written owner authorizations through email/WhatsApp/SMS/other electronic communication, and actions reasonably required to discharge the agreement.

## Core Services

Core services covered by PMC commission include online marketing, dynamic pricing, booking/payment management, guest communication, housekeeping oversight, check-in/check-out coordination, maintenance scheduling/supervision, and monthly owner reporting. Direct repair labour/material costs are owner-chargeable as Other Expenses.

## Cleaning And Owner Stays

- Checkout cleans after guest stays are funded by the Cleaning Fee charged to guests or, where applicable, directly to owners.
- Additional owner-requested cleans can be accepted or declined at manager discretion.
- For owner/owner-guest stays, owner must tell Friday at least 48 hours before checkout whether they self-clean or request professional clean.
- If no preference is given by the 48-hour mark, default to professional clean and charge owner.
- If owner self-cleans but inspection fails, schedule professional clean and charge as Other Expenses.
- Owner/owner-guest stays normally do not incur PMC commission or linen fee when Friday is not expected to provide services. If Friday provides support/coordination/oversight, standard commission and linen fee may apply.

## Spending Authority

Use this for maintenance, repairs, service recovery, and guest-facing gestures:

- Routine limit without prior owner approval: up to the greater of MUR 2,500 or 10% of Total Guest Paid for the reservation that caused the incident, capped at MUR 20,000.
- If expected incident cost is above the routine limit but does not exceed MUR 20,000, request owner written approval before authorizing work.
- If owner does not respond within 24 hours, approval is deemed granted for the specified amount, unless a stricter property-specific rule exists.
- Urgent situation override: if owner is unreachable/non-responsive and immediate action is needed, Friday may act up to MUR 20,000 per incident.
- Amounts above MUR 20,000 always require explicit owner approval.
- Major repairs/upgrades over MUR 20,000 require owner approval. Coordination fee up to 10% plus VAT may apply if Friday coordinates/supervises.

## Urgent Situations

Treat as potentially urgent when delay could cause:

- Guest cancellation or early departure.
- Platform dispute.
- Likely negative review below 5 stars or 10/10.
- Reputational harm, algorithm downgrade, or listing penalty.
- Health, safety, or hygiene risk.
- Financial or reputational damage.

## Records

Every owner-chargeable incident should retain itemized records:

- task/work order link
- vendor or staff
- quote/invoice/receipt
- owner approval state
- urgency reason if override used
- photos where useful
- whether deducted from payout, deducted from deposit, or separately invoiced

## Owner Responsibilities Relevant To Ops

- Owner must maintain legal/safety compliance, structure, fixtures, base furnishings, utilities, water, electricity, internet, and reliable external providers.
- Soft goods and amenities must meet five-star guest standards, including mattresses/protectors, pillows/pillow covers, stable internet, working AC where present, safe electricity, hot/cold water, and appropriate kitchenware.
- If owner-appointed providers create guest-experience risk, Friday may engage an alternative contractor under the Clause 9 process.
- Lockbox/key-safe: Friday may supply, install, maintain, and change codes. Operational lockbox is a service condition. Owner can request current code, but it is not shared by default.

## Service Recovery

Friday may grant discounts, partial refunds, fee waivers, or other adjustments to resolve guest issues or disputes. For Ops scheduling, this means the agent should flag likely service-recovery risk when the task can materially affect guest satisfaction.

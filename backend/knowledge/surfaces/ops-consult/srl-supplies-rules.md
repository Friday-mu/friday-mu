# SRL, Welcome Pack, And Supplies Rules

SRL/welcome items are normally placed during the cleaning stage and verified during post-clean inspection. If cleaning did not place them, post-clean should place them and record the exception.

## Per Bathroom

- Shampoo bottles: 2
- Conditioner bottles: 2
- Soaps: 2
- Passive scent item: 1 per bathroom

## Per Property

- Water bottles in fridge: 2
- Coke can: 1
- Fanta can: 1
- Ferrero Rocher three-pack: 1
- Extra passive scent item: 1 for living room or another suitable area

## Per Max Guest

- Coffee packets: 2 per max guest
- Sugar packets: 2 per max guest

Max guests = bedrooms x 2 plus one per sofa bed.

## Cleaning Product Restock

For detergents and cleaning products, bring refill gallons where appropriate and refill onsite containers. Field task should ask staff to confirm stocked/not stocked instead of assuming.

## Supplies UX

For eligible tasks, Friday Consult should propose calculated supply quantities and ask staff to confirm/edit. Do not silently decrement stock until the staff confirms what was actually placed or used.

## Examples

- 1 bathroom, 2 max guests: shampoo 2, conditioner 2, soap 2, passive scent 2 total, water 2, Coke 1, Fanta 1, Ferrero pack 1, coffee 4, sugar 4.
- 3 bathrooms, 6 max guests: shampoo 6, conditioner 6, soap 6, passive scent 4 total, water 2, Coke 1, Fanta 1, Ferrero pack 1, coffee 12, sugar 12.

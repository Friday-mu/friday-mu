# Task Duration And Skill Matrix

Use 15-minute scheduling blocks. Prefer slight overestimation to underestimation when uncertainty is high.

## Property Sizes

- Small: studio, one-bedroom, and some two-bedroom apartments.
- Medium: most two-bedroom and three-bedroom apartments/penthouses; some villas when exterior is not large.
- Large: most three-bedroom and four-bedroom properties, villas, houses, and properties with substantial exterior/pool/complexity.
- Combo properties are parent bookings but should break into child tasks:
  - LB-C = LB-1, LB-2, LB-3. Treat as three medium units.
  - VA-C = VA-1, VA-2, VA-3, VA-4. VA-1 and VA-2 are medium; VA-3 and VA-4 are small.

## Max Guest Rule

- Base capacity = bedrooms x 2.
- Sofa bed adds 1 guest.
- Four bedrooms normally means 8 guests.

## Cleaning And Inspection Durations

- Standard clean: small 90 min, medium 120 min, large 150 min.
- Owner standard clean: same as standard clean.
- Mid-stay clean: same as standard clean.
- Deep clean: 2x standard clean.
- Post-clean inspection: small 15 min, medium 30 min, large 45 min.
- Owner post-clean inspection: same as post-clean inspection.
- Arrival inspection: small 30 min, medium 30 min, large 45 min.
- Aesthetic check: small 15 min, medium 30 min, large 45 min.

## Recurring Property Work

- Amenities report: small 60 min, medium 75 min, large 90 min.
- Preventative maintenance: small 90 min, medium 120 min, large 150 min. AC filter cleaning is included. Better to reschedule than assign to Ishant because it is non-urgent.
- Lockbox code change: 15 min.
- Guesty lockbox update: 15 min.
- Pest control: 15 min max per property.
- Store cleaning: 60 min, every two weeks per store.
- Home buildout: small 60 min, medium 75 min, large 90 min.
- AC servicing: 60 min per AC, external vendor for now. Bryan may do this in future if trained.

## Maintenance And Procurement

- Minimum maintenance block: 15 min.
- Quick reset: 15 min. If in west and Bryan is north, Ishant can handle super simple resets.
- Medium/complex maintenance: estimate by issue type, then add buffer. If uncertain, draft a Bryan assessment task before scheduling full repair.
- Procurement: travel time plus at least 30 min at store/vendor. Ishant is west car backup. Mathias is north car backup.

## Skill Assignment

- Maintenance: Bryan first. If external vendor is needed, Bryan supervises or assesses.
- Cleaning: Catherine and Bryan first. Franny only emergency. Ishant should not be assigned routine cleaning.
- Inspections/post-clean: Catherine, Bryan, Franny if needed, Ishant west backup.
- Amenities/home buildout/aesthetic: Catherine, Bryan, Franny, Mathias north backup, Ishant west backup only where appropriate.
- Owner comms/admin/Guesty updates: Franny, Mathias, Mary while active, Ishant for escalations.

## Booking Triggers

- Guest checkout triggers standard clean and post-clean inspection on checkout date.
- If the reservation moves, linked checkout tasks should move.
- Arrival inspection happens two days before arrival unless the property was cleaned within the last two days. Default to creating it for safety, then cancel if confirmed unnecessary.
- Owner blocks: default to arranging cleaning unless owner confirms self-clean at least 48 hours before checkout. If self-clean fails inspection, create chargeable cleaning.
- Long stays over 14 days: free mid-stay clean for medium and large properties only, generally around the midpoint of each two-week stay.

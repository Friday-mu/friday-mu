# Tone Cadence

## Stage-by-Stage Tone Guide

### Stage 1: Inquiry
- **Tone**: Warm, enthusiastic, helpful — match their energy
- **Example**: "Thank you for your interest in [property]! We'd love to help you plan your perfect stay."

### Stage 2: 3-Hour Follow-up
- **Tone**: Friendly, informative — add value, don't just ask "still interested?"
- **Example**: "Just checking in — I noticed you were looking at [property] for [dates]. Still considering?"

### Stage 3: 12-Hour Follow-up
- **Tone**: Personal, low-pressure — reference specifics from their inquiry
- **Example**: "Hi [name], following up on your inquiry. Happy to answer any questions about [property] or the area."

### Stage 4: 24-Hour Follow-up
- **Tone**: Professional, gentle urgency — mention popularity or limited availability
- **Example**: "I know you're probably weighing options — just wanted to let you know [property] is getting quite popular for your dates."

### Stage 5: 1-Week Follow-up
- **Tone**: Respectful closing — offer alternatives, leave door open
- **Example**: "Last note from me — if [property] isn't quite right, we have some other options that might be a better fit."

## General Selling Points

- Curated collection of premium properties across Mauritius
- Personal concierge service from booking to checkout
- Local team on the ground for any issues
- Direct communication — no waiting for platform message relay
- Flexible booking terms
- Authentic Mauritius experiences — local tips and recommendations

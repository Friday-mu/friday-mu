# Inquiry Followup

## 5-Stage Cadence

1. **Initial (inquiry)** — Warm, enthusiastic, helpful. Acknowledge interest, highlight property strengths, offer to answer questions.
2. **Follow-up 3h** — Friendly, informative. Add value (local tips, seasonal highlights), gentle availability reminder.
3. **Follow-up 12h** — Personal, low-pressure. Reference specifics from inquiry.
4. **Follow-up 24h** — Professional, gentle urgency. Mention popularity or limited availability.
5. **Follow-up 1w** — Respectful closing. Offer alternatives, leave door open.

## Selling Points

- Curated premium properties across Mauritius
- Personal concierge service from booking to checkout
- Local team on the ground
- Direct communication — no platform delays
- Flexible booking terms
- Authentic local experiences and recommendations

## Objection Handling

- **Price concern**: Value framing, not discounting. Highlight what's included, compare to hotels, mention FRIDAY10 for returning guests.
- **Availability**: Offer alternatives immediately. Suggest similar properties, flexible dates, waitlist.
- **Location questions**: Paint the experience. Proximity to beaches, transport, local restaurants, neighborhood character.
- **Comparison shopping**: Differentiate on service. Personal concierge, local expertise, quality guarantee, flexible check-in/out.

# Legal Review Boundary

## Allowed Before Review

- Summarize official source packets with source dates.
- Identify missing documents or review questions.
- Draft non-binding internal candidate wording.
- Create compliance checklist candidates.
- Route action requests to the owning human reviewer.

## Not Allowed Before Review

- Final legal advice.
- Contract commitments or changed owner/guest terms.
- Filing or license completion claims.
- Tax/legal/regulatory interpretation as final Friday policy.
- Any public or owner-facing legal wording not approved by Ishant or the designated reviewer.

## Response Pattern

When legal/admin risk is present:

1. Name the source and source date if known.
2. Separate fact from interpretation.
3. State what is missing.
4. Draft the review packet.
5. Do not promise an external outcome.

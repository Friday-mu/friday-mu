# Ask Friday - Legal/Admin

This is a planned KB shell. It does not authorize legal advice, contract commitments, regulatory filings, or compliance changes.

## Mission

Help staff assemble legal/admin source packets, draft internal candidates, track compliance questions, and identify review needs without giving unsupported legal advice or external commitments.

## Source Truth

1. Friday-approved contract templates, owner agreements, annexes, policies, and compliance calendar.
2. Official Mauritius sources for tourism accommodation licensing, MRA/tourist-fee references, data protection, employment law, and corporate filings.
3. Staff-reviewed legal/admin decisions and accepted ADRs.
4. External counsel or authorized reviewer output before legal wording becomes canonical.

## Non-Goals

- Do not provide final legal advice.
- Do not commit Friday to contract terms, owner obligations, guest obligations, tax positions, licensing outcomes, or regulatory interpretations.
- Do not alter contracts, approvals, licenses, or compliance records automatically.
- Do not expose restricted legal, HR, owner, guest, payment, or staff-sensitive data outside its audience.

## Answer Rules

- Distinguish official source facts, Friday policy, legal interpretation, and assumptions.
- For contracts, produce review packets or candidate wording, not final binding terms.
- For tourism accommodation and licensing, use source-dated Tourism Authority or Laws of Mauritius references.
- For data protection, use DPO/Mauritius Data Protection Act sources and route operational decisions to review.
- Refresh licensing, corporate-registration, data-protection, and employment-law source rows quarterly or before any external answer.
- For HR/legal overlap, keep HR-sensitive facts restricted.

## Review Required

- Final list of legal/admin source owners.
- Which templates Ask Friday may summarize versus draft against.
- Who approves contract wording, license/compliance actions, data-protection policy, and owner/legal commitments.
- Xodo Sign/document-signature wiring and which signed artifacts are source of truth.

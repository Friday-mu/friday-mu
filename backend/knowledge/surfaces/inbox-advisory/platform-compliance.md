# Platform Compliance

## Airbnb

- All communication through platform until booking confirmed
- After confirmation: may share direct contact within thread
- Never ask guests to communicate off-platform before confirmation
- Never mention cheaper direct prices
- No sharing links to competing platforms
- No discriminatory language
- No requesting payments outside Airbnb
- No requesting reviews or offering incentives
- No sharing other guests' personal information

## Cancellation (Airbnb)

- Flexible: Full refund up to 24h before
- Moderate: Full refund up to 5 days; 50% after
- Strict: Full refund within 48h of booking if ≥14 days before; 50% if ≥7 days; none after
- Host cancellation penalties: first = $50 + blocked dates; repeat = $100 + Superhost revoked

## Booking.com

- Defer to platform terms for cancellation
- Under 4h response for Quick Response badge

## Direct Booking

- 30 days before = full refund; inside 30 days = no refund
- Payment details sent automatically at inquiry time
- Do not inline bank details in drafts

## Cross-Platform

- Maintain price parity
- Calendar sync automatic via Guesty

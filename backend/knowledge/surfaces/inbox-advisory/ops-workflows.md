# Operational Workflows

## Maintenance Dispatch

1. Guest reports issue or team identifies it
2. Franny assesses urgency
3. Bryan dispatched (north and west)
4. West: Alexandra coordinates on-site access
5. Above Rs 5,000: Franny escalates to Ishant (or Mathias if Ishant unavailable)
6. Issue resolved, guest notified
7. Case logged in reservation notes

## Owner Communication

- Philosophy: Minimal, only essentials. Passive ownership experience.
- Monthly: Statements sent within first 7 days of following month
- Day-to-day: Per-owner WhatsApp group (Mary, Mathias, Ishant, Franny + owner)
- Below threshold: handled silently, no owner notification
- Above threshold: owner approval via WhatsApp group
- Pricing and listings: Friday decides, no owner approval
- Spending above threshold: owner approval required

## Check-in / Check-out

- Standard check-in: 3 PM
- Standard checkout: 10 AM
- Breezeway auto-creates cleaning + inspection tasks
- Post-clean: place SRL items (shampoo, body gel, soap, laundry detergent, chocolate, water, Coke, Fanta, sugar, salt)
- Post-clean inspection by Bryan/Alex

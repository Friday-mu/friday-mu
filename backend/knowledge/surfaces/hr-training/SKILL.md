# Ask Friday - HR/Training

This is a planned KB shell. It can guide SOP/training design but does not authorize HR decisions, staff discipline, compensation changes, or individual performance profiling.

## Mission

Help staff find SOPs, training material, role expectations, roster/leave context, and safe operational guidance while keeping HR/private performance data restricted.

## Source Truth

1. Approved Friday SOPs, role checklists, training modules, and skill matrix.
2. Roster/leave data from FAD HR/Ops sources.
3. Staff profile, role, and training progress only when role-scoped and authorized.
4. Official Mauritius labour/employment sources for high-level source packets, not final HR advice.

## Non-Goals

- Do not make hiring, firing, discipline, performance, compensation, leave, or legal employment decisions.
- Do not expose individual staff performance, private HR notes, medical data, complaints, or disciplinary data outside approved HR lanes.
- Do not use Ops productivity data to rank staff without an approved HR policy.

## Answer Rules

- Prefer SOP/training answers over HR conclusions.
- Keep staff-sensitive data restricted and audience-scoped.
- For leave, hours, overtime, breaks, or employment rights, use source-dated official references and route final policy to HR/legal review.
- Refresh labour-law, payroll contribution, training levy, and HRDC source rows quarterly or before any external/staff-policy answer.
- When Ops and HR overlap, use only the minimum staff data needed for scheduling and do not include private HR notes.

## Review Required

- Final HR privacy policy and reviewer roles.
- Dedicated restricted HR review lane for training, leave, performance, payroll, discipline, and staff-sensitive candidates.
- Which training/progress data Ask Friday can access per role.
- Whether individual performance analytics are forbidden, HR-only, or allowed in limited reviewed cases.
- Approved source list for Mauritius employment-law references.

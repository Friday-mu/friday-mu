# SOP And Training Matrix

## Minimum SOP Metadata

- `sopId`
- `title`
- `role`
- `module`
- `version`
- `owner`
- `lastReviewedAt`
- `privacyClass`
- `sourcePath`
- `relatedEvalIds`

## Training Guidance Rules

- Use the latest approved SOP version.
- If SOP is missing or stale, create a candidate instead of inventing the rule.
- Keep training guidance practical and role-specific.
- Do not include guest-sensitive, owner-private, or restricted staff data in examples unless authorized and redacted.

## Initial SOP Domains

- Inbox guest reply drafting.
- Ops task creation and closure.
- Roster and schedule review.
- Feedback/bug reporting.
- Property data correction.
- Owner approval routing.

**Stop juggling Rayon Design, spreadsheets, WhatsApp threads, and 12

## The conversational floor-plan editor

The thing that nobody else has.

Today you redraw walls in Rayon. Then you fight a single-shot AI to
add furniture — every prompt redraws the whole layout. So you stop
using the AI and place every piece manually.

In FridayOS Design:

1. Upload the scanned plan
2. Trace the walls / doors / windows once (10 minutes max)
3. Then **just talk to it**:
   - "Add a sofa in the living room"
   - "Move the sofa to the left wall"
   - "Change the wall colour to navy"
   - "Switch the dining table for something rounder"
4. Each turn keeps the walls in place. Furniture moves; structure doesn't.
5. Save the version you like as final.

You're not babysitting a single-shot generator. You're iterating with
a tool that knows what a floor plan IS.
